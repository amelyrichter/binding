What even is

"bad"

design?

The following three articles talk about how the idea of "good" design is ever changing. Modern design starts evolving when it breaks away from old rules, embracing things like chaos, weirdness, and even ugliness to challenge norms. But at the same time, they all remind us that design still needs to do its job—communicating clearly and meaningfully—no matter how wild or rebellious it looks.
